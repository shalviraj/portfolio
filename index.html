About

I'm a UX designer specialising in complex, domain-specific software. Over the last four years I've been embedded in Pilz's product team in Cork, Ireland, working on Myzel — a platform that helps safety engineers manage the full lifecycle of industrial machines.

My work spans user research, strategy, end-to-end product design, and cross-team delivery. I care most about understanding the domain deeply before designing anything — and about making complex processes feel manageable for the people who rely on them every day.

When I'm not designing, I'm usually at a UX conference, completing a course, or thinking about how to make better tools for people who take safety seriously.
