Read more about me →

I'm a UX designer specialising in complex, domain-specific software. Four years embedded in Pilz's product team, working on Myzel — a platform that helps safety engineers manage the full lifecycle of industrial machines.

My work spans research, strategy, and end-to-end product design. I care most about understanding a domain deeply before designing anything.

What I bring
Domain-first research

I don't design before I understand. User interviews, journey maps, and domain research are where every project starts — especially when the field is specialised and the users are experts.

End-to-end ownership

From HLR review to post-launch iteration, I've owned the full design process across a complex multi-module platform — coordinating across teams, validating with users, and shipping to 28 countries.

Design that ships

I work closely with development teams to ensure designs are implementable, consistent, and scalable. Usability testing, heuristic audits, and design system migration are part of how I make sure quality lands in production.
